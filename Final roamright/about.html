About RoamRight

Born in Gujarat, built for the world — we make travel simple, beautiful, and unforgettable.

Our Mission

Travel Shouldn't Be Complicated

RoamRight was founded on a simple belief: that planning a trip should be just as exciting as taking one. Too many travelers waste hours juggling multiple apps, disconnected information, and confusing booking flows. We set out to change that.

Our platform brings together destination discovery, trip planning, and seamless booking into one beautifully designed experience — so you spend less time planning and more time exploring.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

2021

The Idea

RoamRight was born out of frustration with complex travel apps during a backpacking trip through Rajasthan.

2022

First Design

Our core team of four completed the first wireframes and UI prototypes, focusing on a search-first philosophy.

2023

Beta Launch

We launched our beta with 20 destinations and 500 early users who helped shape our product through feedback.

2024

Full Launch

RoamRight officially launched with 120+ destinations, the Trip Planner, and 50,000+ happy travelers.

2025

Growing Strong

Continuing to grow with new features, more destinations, and a mobile app coming soon.
